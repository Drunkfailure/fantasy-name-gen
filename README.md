# The DnD Name Generator

A fantasy name generator that includes options for many races for the playable and encounterable races you will traditionally find in fantasy media such as human, elf, dwarf, goblin, tiefling, dragonborn. Currently stocked with over 2000 first and last names and growing, with more races to be added soon.

Forked from https://github.com/Lukewh/fantasy-name.

## Examples and Themes of Racial Names

### Humans
**Theme:** Classic, versatile names from various cultures
- Often inspired by medieval European, Middle Eastern, or Asian naming conventions
- Can be simple and common, or exotic and unique
- Last names often reflect occupations, locations, or family heritage

**Examples:**
- First Names: Aiden, Marcus, Elena, Sophia, Kael, Thorne
- Last Names: Blackwood, Heath, Gray, Shadowbane, Redman
***
### Elves
**Theme:** Elegant, flowing, melodic names
- Soft consonants and vowels (L, R, N, S, A, E, I)
- Often longer and more lyrical
- Last names may reference nature, light, or ancient heritage

**Examples:**
- First Names: Arwen, Galadriel, Elrond, Liriel, Thranduil
- Last Names: Zelion, Valandir, Taelion, Daraeth
***
### Dwarf
**Theme:** Strong, guttural sounds with hard consonants
- Heavy use of consonants like K, G, R, D, B
- Often short and punchy
- Last names frequently reference stone, metal, or craftsmanship

**Examples:**
- First Names: Thorgrim, Balin, Durin, Gimli, Hilda, Kira
- Last Names: Ironforge, Stonehammer, Goldbeard, Anvilborn, Rockbreaker
***
### Orc
**Theme:** Harsh, aggressive sounds with strong consonants
- Guttural sounds (G, K, R, Z, X)
- Often short and powerful
- Last names may reference war, strength, or tribal affiliations

**Examples:**
- First Names: Grom, Karg, Zog, Ragnar, Urz, Thak
- Last Names: Skullcrusher, Bloodaxe, Ironjaw, Warhammer, Deathclaw
***
### Tiefling
**Theme:** Exotic, infernal-sounding names with a mix of elegance and darkness
- Often contain X, Z, or unusual letter combinations
- Can be melodic but with a darker edge
- Last names may reference infernal heritage or mysterious origins

**Examples:**
- First Names: Zephyr, Xara, Malachi, Lilith, Asmodeus, Nyx
- Last Names: Qazvex, Othrazel, Fothiel, Xarathiel, Belvex
***
### Goblin
**Theme:** Short, quirky, sometimes comical names
- Often one or two syllables
- May contain unusual consonant clusters
- Can be playful or mischievous-sounding
- Last names might reference mischief or small things

**Examples:**
- First Names: Grik, Snix, Zog, Nix, Krik, Tix
- Last Names: Quickfoot, Smalltooth, Sneakysneak, Tinyclaw, Swiftfinger
***
### Dragonborn
**Theme:** Powerful, draconic-sounding names
- Often contain hard consonants and strong vowels
- May reference dragons, scales, or elemental forces
- Can be longer and more imposing
- Last names frequently reference draconic heritage or elements

**Examples:**
- First Names: Drakthar, Vexx, Kythara, Zephyrion, Thrax, Saphira
- Last Names: Scaleshield, Firebreath, Stormwing, Ironclaw, Goldscale
***
### General Notes

- **Gender Variations:** Female names often have softer endings (-a, -ia, -elle), while male names may have harder endings (-ar, -or, -ak)
- **Cultural Blending:** Many names blend elements from multiple real-world cultures to create unique fantasy identities
- **Meaning:** Names often reflect the character's background, personality, or role in the story
- **Flexibility:** The generator allows for both traditional and creative name combinations

## Usage

![gif of generator](.github/DnD-names.gif)


- Use Ctrl - P on Windows to open commands and type 'name' to pull up all possible combinations of random names.
- You can customize hotkeys for all racial choices in the settings of the plugin.

_This plugin calls a supabase database to generate the names._
